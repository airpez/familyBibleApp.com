Sacred Echo

A spiritual home you can hear

The Family Bible App is an audio heirloom of Scripture — keeping relationships strong and preserving the voices of the ones you love long after the moment has passed.

Each person records passages, and the app indexes every reading by book, chapter, and verse. Shareable links, guided plans, and verse-by-verse playback in the voices you know best. Build something sacred, personal, and private — wisdom that's always with you, ready to pass down.

"The deepest wisdom, in the voices that shaped you."

Record. Listen. Pass it on — one verse at a time.

The Experience

Record. Listen. Together.

Three simple acts that compound into a legacy your grandchildren will treasure.

01 — Record

Your voice, verse by verse

Pick any passage and record on a beautiful cassette-style interface. Smart "where to start" logic picks up where you left off. Review each verse before sharing.

02 — Listen

Chapters that flow like a podcast

Open a chapter and hear it stitched continuously across every family member. Filter by reader, resume where you left off, and trim silence for tight playback.

03 — Together

A family project that grows

Invite grandparents, siblings, and cousins. Track collective Bible coverage, follow guided plans, share study notes, and celebrate streaks together.

Everything you need

Built for real families

Not another Bible app — a personal, voice-based heirloom with thoughtful features at every step.

Full Bible, two translations

World English Bible and Berean Standard Bible — all 66 books, every verse. Choose your preferred translation for reading and recording.

Guided Bible plans

Follow structured recording and listening plans with progress tracking. Build habits that lead to a complete family audio Bible over time.

Family study notes

Add verse-level notes with comments your whole family can see. Share insights, questions, and reflections as you grow together.

Badges & streaks

Celebrate milestones and maintain recording streaks. A dashboard keeps everyone motivated and shows how far your family has come.

Shareable links

Invite family members with polished link previews. Every new voice strengthens your collective audio Bible and deepens connection.

Private by design

Your family's recordings stay within your family group. Secure authentication, encrypted storage, and admin controls you can trust.
